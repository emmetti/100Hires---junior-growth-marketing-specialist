# Competitive Gap Analysis: 100hires vs. Legacy ATS (Workable)

## The Incumbent Advantage

Legacy platforms like Workable rely heavily on Domain Rating (DR) and an aged backlog of manual content. Their organic moat is built on legacy backlinks.

## The AI Pipeline Opportunity

Legacy platforms are currently too bottlenecked by legal and brand compliance to deploy AI content at scale. Their programmatic pages (like job descriptions) are static.

## The 100hires Playbook Execution

By utilizing the architectural frameworks outlined in this research repository, 100hires can bypass the need for traditional blogging.

1. **Extraction (Mike King's model):** Pull real-time salary and skill data from market APIs.
2. **Structuring (Preetam Nath's model):** Feed that data into strict LLM templates.
3. **Internal Linking (Kevin Indig's model):** Interlink new programmatic pages dynamically to force rapid indexing.

This allows 100hires to out-publish legacy competitors by a factor of 100:1 without triggering Google's Helpful Content penalties (guarded by Lily Ray's E-E-A-T frameworks).
