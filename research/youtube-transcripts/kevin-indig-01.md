### [00:00]
For marketplace or database-driven sites, organic growth is not driven by the blog; it is driven by the taxonomy. The way you categorize and interlink your inventory—in this case, job data and hiring resources—determines your indexation rate.
### [02:50]
When you apply AI to this, the focus should be on building dynamic internal link graphs. The LLM can analyze the relationship between different job categories and generate highly relevant contextual links between pages that a human would miss. This flattens the site architecture, allowing search engines to discover and index deep programmatic pages much faster, creating a compounding growth loop.
