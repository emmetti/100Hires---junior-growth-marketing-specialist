### [00:00]
The rift: since March 2025, organic traffic and organic rankings have decoupled. AI overviews now appear on approximately 50% of non-branded keywords for the largest sites on the web. Pew Research confirmed click-through rates on organic results are cut in half when AI overviews appear, and only 1% of users click links within the AIO itself. AI mode shows nearly 100% zero-click share. These are not traffic drivers — they are answer environments.

### [11:30]
User behavior studies across 70 participants reveal that trust beats relevance as the primary filter. Before users ask whether a result answers their question, they ask whether they trust the source. Brand recognition is the number one evaluation criterion. Implications for B2B SaaS: if you are not in the minds of your users before they search, you are working uphill. Being present on UGC platforms like Reddit matters because users validate AI answers there — when AI overviews appear, UGC platform clicks jump from 18% to 30% of final clicks.

### [23:45]
Additive content — the opposite of evergreen — is the new competitive moat. LLMs can answer evergreen questions. What they cannot answer is new, original, firsthand research and thought leadership. The highest-performing content strategy identified came from analyzing sales call transcripts: extracting real prospect questions, fears, and objections, then building content around those topics. This produced significantly higher conversion rates than classic keyword-volume-driven content because it matches in-market intent precisely.

### [35:00]
Freshness is more important for LLM citation than for classic search. Content younger than three months is cited significantly more often. Adding a visible last-updated date to landing pages across a large site tripled citation rate. LLMs prioritize fresh content because they rely on live web retrieval for topics outside their training cutoff. Definitive, clear writing style (no fluff, specific entity naming) produces almost 2x more citations than vague or narrative-heavy content.

### [47:15]
Web reputation — how often and how you are mentioned across the web — is the single most important factor for AI visibility across ChatGPT, Gemini, and Perplexity. Quality of mentions matters far more than volume. Reviews on G2, Trustpilot, and Google Maps create a consensus signal that LLMs use to validate brand claims. The new playbook: visibility (additive content + freshness + style + reputation) combined with trust (evidence + experts + validation + brand reputation) replaces the classic click-funnel with an influence model across multiple platforms simultaneously.
