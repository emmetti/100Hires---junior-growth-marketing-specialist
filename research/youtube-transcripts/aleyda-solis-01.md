### [00:00]
Managing a site with thousands of programmatic pages requires shifting from manual audits to automated AI agents. When you are deploying AI-generated pages at volume, your internal linking and crawl architecture can become bloated very quickly. You end up with crawl waste, where Googlebot is wasting resources on low-value pagination instead of your core landing pages.
### [04:10]
You need to set up automated workflows that monitor server logs and check for canonicalization issues in real-time as new pages are published. The AI should be deployed to classify crawl errors and automatically suggest the precise technical fix, whether that is updating the robots.txt or adjusting the programmatic internal linking rules.
