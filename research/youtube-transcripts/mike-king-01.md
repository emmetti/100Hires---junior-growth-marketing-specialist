### [00:00]
The actual power of generative AI in SEO isn't writing; it's extraction and structuring at scale. We are looking at workflows where we pull thousands of unstructured job postings via API and use an LLM not to rewrite them, but to extract specific entities like required certifications, years of experience, and software proficiencies.
### [02:15]
Once you have structured that data into a JSON or database format, you can programmatically inject it into your page templates. This allows a platform to spin up highly specific, data-rich aggregator pages that outrank competitors who are just manually writing broad overview articles. You're using AI for data science, not copywriting.
