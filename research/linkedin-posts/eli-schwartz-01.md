# Stop Optimizing for Keywords, Optimize for the Product
Everyone is panicking about Answer Engine Optimization (AEO) and AI Overviews stealing clicks. 
Here is the reality for SaaS: If your SEO strategy relies on top-of-funnel informational queries that an LLM can answer in two sentences, your traffic is already dead. 
The survival strategy is Product-Led SEO. 
Build the SEO into the utility of the product. If you run an Applicant Tracking System, don't write generic "How to hire" blogs. Surface your anonymized hiring data. Create programmatic hubs of real job descriptions, salary benchmarks, and interview questions that require the user to interact with your platform to get the full value. 
Google can scrape a blog. It cannot scrape an interactive product experience.
