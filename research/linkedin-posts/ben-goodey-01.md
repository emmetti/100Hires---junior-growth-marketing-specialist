# Why Legacy SaaS Companies are Losing Organic Share
I just finished tearing down the organic strategies of the top 3 legacy ATS platforms. The data is brutal. They rely entirely on domain authority and backlinks built in 2018. Their content velocity is stagnant.
If you are a challenger SaaS brand today, you don't beat them by writing "better" blog posts. You beat them on architecture and coverage. 
A startup using AI to generate targeted, high-utility programmatic pages (e.g., specific interview questions for specific micro-roles) can outflank a legacy platform in months, not years. The incumbents are too slow to deploy AI at scale because of internal legal/brand bottlenecks. That is the exact gap where aggressive B2B growth happens.
