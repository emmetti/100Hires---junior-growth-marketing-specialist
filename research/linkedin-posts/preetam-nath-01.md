# Programmatic SEO Course for 2026: Grow Your Startup Using pSEO

Programmatic SEO is a methodical and data-driven approach to finding keywords, understanding user intent, and creating dozens or hundreds of pages of content.

**SEO has 2 key components:**
1. Understand what people are trying to find.
2. Create content that answers their questions.

If you're a product builder, you're already aware of this mindset. Instead of building features, you create content.

**What is Programmatic SEO?**
It's a method for addressing a large number of search terms or questions that people might be looking for on Google, by generating individual landing pages or blog posts answering each of those questions.

Key components:
- Identifying repeatable search terms
- Understanding user intent — what is the user looking for
- Generating pages manually or programmatically that address their questions

**Real results:** Using these principles, DelightChat grew search impressions from 600/mo to 240,000/mo in 90 days.

**The pSEO framework applied to B2B SaaS:**
The Zapier case study is instructive — they found 25,000 landing page ideas and scaled to millions in search traffic by identifying every possible integration combination (Tool A + Tool B) and generating a dedicated page for each. The pattern: Variable 1 x Variable 2 = scalable page matrix.

For an ATS platform, the same logic applies directly. Role x Location. Interview Questions x Job Title. Salary Benchmark x Industry. Each combination is a discrete search intent with a user who needs a specific answer. The product data already exists. The pSEO framework turns that data into an organic acquisition engine.

The creativity requirement is forming the analogy from case study to your specific product. The execution is generating the matrix and building the pages. Scale comes from the system, not from writing individual pieces of content.
