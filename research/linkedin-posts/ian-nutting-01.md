# Traffic is a Vanity Metric in B2B
AI has made top-of-funnel (TOFU) traffic cheaper to generate than ever before. If your playbook is just pumping out informational glossaries, congratulations, you have a lot of traffic that will never buy your software.
The focus must shift entirely to Bottom-of-Funnel (BOFU) intent. 
When generating content at scale, the prompt architecture must include specific conversion triggers. If a user lands on a programmatic page for "Customer Success Manager Job Description," the page needs to instantly pivot to "Here is how to track this candidate pipeline in our tool." 
Connect the AI output directly to the product's core value proposition, or you are just doing free research for your competitors.
