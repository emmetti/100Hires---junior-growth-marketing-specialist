# Does Traffic from AI Platforms Convert "Better" Than Google Search Traffic?

Everyone is pushing a different narrative. And there's a "study" to support every storyline. The reality: it's all segment specific.

**Real-world client example (B2B SaaS): AI search converts 50% better than Google & Bing on-site — but drives far lower deal values.**

In the trailing 12 months, one mid-market B2B SaaS client showed a stark difference:

- Organic Search best month: $344,911 ARR
- AI Search best month: $33,000 ARR
- Organic Search total: $1.2M ARR
- AI Search total: $85K ARR

The 37x difference in average deal size demonstrates that organic search is attributable to more qualified, enterprise-level buyers ready for substantial investments, while AI leads typically yield smaller, exploratory purchases.

**Why does AI search drive higher website conversion rates?**
ChatGPT is built for fulfilling very specific intent. Visitors arriving from AI platforms are better informed due to the hyper-personalized, conversational search journey. ChatGPT barely sends traffic for informational intent — by default, most outgoing AI traffic is commercial intent, so conversion rates are higher despite lower volume.

**Why does Google drive higher B2B deal sizes despite lower conversion rates?**
Most B2B SEO strategies were built on driving informational TOFU traffic to inflate organic numbers. Top-of-funnel content strategies attract low buying intent. ChatGPT already has the answer to informational questions in its training data — its goal is to give answers, not drive traffic.

**The conversion pattern emerging for B2B SaaS:**
AI = top of funnel discovery, exploration, narrowing.
Google + Brand/Direct = conversion.

Zero-click conversions are the missing piece. Most answers that cause conversions are zero click — buyers head to Google and search the brand to convert after research is done elsewhere. The modern search path from discovery to conversion has become insanely fragmented, and SEO attribution is broken in the AI era.

The practical implication for ATS platforms: build content that wins AI discovery for exploratory hiring queries, then ensure brand search captures the conversion. The pipeline connects — it just doesn't show up in last-click attribution.
